Read this before you submit

  • You're direct to the seller. No daisy chains, no passing along someone else's deal.
  • Not listed on the MLS and no realtor in the middle (novations are the exception, that's their whole play).
  • Know your numbers before you submit. ARV, repairs, and contract price are the three we judge everything on.
  • Real deals only. If you wouldn't put your own money on it, don't put your name on it.

Questions everybody asks

What's the split?
Straight 50/50. You found the deal, we sell it, we split the fee right down the middle. It goes in the JV agreement in writing before your deal moves an inch, and it doesn't change after. No surprises.
Do I need my own buyers?
No. That's the whole point. You bring the deal, we bring the buyers.
Will you go around me to my seller?
Never. Your contract stays your contract, and we don't touch your seller. The JV agreement locks that in writing before anything moves. We make money when you make money, that's the deal.
What kind of deals do you take?
Cash deals, creative (sub-to and seller finance), and novations. Houses first. If you're not sure yours fits, submit it anyway and let us tell you.
My contract expires soon. Should I still submit?
Yes, and tell us the date. A short runway kills more deals than bad numbers do, so the sooner you send it, the better your shot.
How do I know what's happening with my deal?
You'll hear from us, not crickets. We tell you when it's reviewed, when it's in front of buyers, and when there's an offer. If it's not moving, we tell you that too.
